Day 4 — Next day Pure value · No pitch · Build goodwill
Goal: Deliver Value
Subject Line
3 mistakes that keep beginners broke online (avoid these)

Hey [First Name],

Today, no tools to recommend. No links to click. Just something I wish someone had told me when I was starting out.

Here are the 3 biggest mistakes I see beginners make — and how to avoid them:

Mistake #1: Trying too many things at once.
Most people jump from model to model — affiliate marketing one week, dropshipping the next, then a course, then print-on-demand. The result? Progress on nothing. Pick one model. Give it 90 days before deciding it doesn't work.

Mistake #2: Waiting until everything is "perfect."
Your first landing page will be ugly. Your first email will be awkward. That's fine. Done is better than perfect. You learn by doing, not by planning.

Mistake #3: Skipping the email list.
Social media algorithms change. Platforms ban accounts. Your email list is the only audience you truly own. Start building it from day one — even if it's just 10 people.

Avoid these three and you'll already be ahead of 90% of people who try to make money online.

Tomorrow I've got something important to share about scaling beyond your first $500/month.
